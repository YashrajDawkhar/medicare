About Our Foundation

Dedicated to improving healthcare access and outcomes for all communities around the world.

Our Mission

The MediCare Foundation is dedicated to bringing essential healthcare to underserved communities. We believe that access to quality healthcare is a fundamental right, not a privilege. Through our network of medical professionals, we provide medical services, education, and resources to communities that need it most.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone, regardless of geographic location or economic status, has access to quality healthcare. Our foundation works toward eliminating healthcare disparities and ensuring that medical innovations benefit all of humanity.

Dr. Robert Chen

Founder & Chairman

Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare programs designed to address diverse medical needs in underserved communities.

Medical Clinics

We operate free and low-cost medical clinics in areas with limited healthcare access, providing routine check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for common illnesses.

Learn more

Medication Programs

Our medication assistance programs help patients access essential prescription drugs at reduced or no cost, ensuring they receive the medications they need.

Learn more

Specialist Care

We coordinate with specialists to provide expert care for chronic and complex conditions, including cardiology, endocrinology, and oncology services.

Learn more

Health Education

We provide educational programs on preventive care, healthy lifestyles, and disease management, empowering communities to take control of their health.

Learn more

Emergency Relief

Our rapid response teams provide medical assistance following natural disasters and other emergencies, delivering critical care when it's needed most.

Learn more

Medical Training

We train local healthcare workers in underserved regions, building capacity and ensuring sustainable healthcare solutions for the future.

Learn more
